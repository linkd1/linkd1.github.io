What is the book about?


This book I am making is about the Backrooms. I looked up 'Backrooms Book' out of curiosity and I found that there were hardly any books, and none of them were any good. The main character was not assigned a name, and so he is referred to as 'X'. He is a Partypooper, a nearly extinct race of entity which was nearly wiped out by Partygoers. The Partygoers were believed to look like party statues but they were instead horrifying warped humans with extremely large smiles. Partygoers like to rip people up and turn them into other Partygoers. Partypoopers, on the other hand, are blue, and shorter than normal humans. They are a negotiating species and do not like to fight, hence why they were nearly wiped out fully.
